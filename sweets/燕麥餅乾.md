# Oatmeal Cookies

## Ingredient

other

- dried cranberries 100g (1 cup)
- 1 cup hot water

batter

- light brown sugar 200g (less sweet 100g)
- butter 170g
- egg 1
- vanilla extract 1 1/2t

dry part
- all purpose flour 100g
- rolled oats 270g
- salt 1/2t
- baking soda 1/2t
- cinnamon 1/2t
- nutmeg 1/8t

## Baking

- 350F 10-15 min
- stay and cool on the paper for 10min before moving to cooling rack.
